UCI ZotBotics - Robot Arm

By Timothy Cho

Project Overview

As part of a team project, we designed, 3D printed, and assembled the BCN3D Moveo robotic arm. Our work involved fabricating the arm's structural components using 3D printing, wiring and connecting stepper motors to their drivers, and programming an Arduino to control the arm’s movement. Together, we developed the functionality for the robot to play rock-paper-scissors autonomously, resulting in us winning 1st place in a rock-paper-scissors competition.
