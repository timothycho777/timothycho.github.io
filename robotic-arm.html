UCI ZotBotics - Robot Arm

By Timothy Cho

Project Overview

The BCN3D Moveo robotic arm was designed and assembled as part of a team project for lab automation. Our work involved 3D printing the structural components, wiring and connecting stepper motors to their drivers, and programming an Arduino to control the arm's movements. As part of the team, I designed the CAD model of the robotic hand using SolidWorks. Together, we developed the functionality for the robot to play rock-paper-scissors autonomously, winning 1st place in a rock-paper-scissors competition.
